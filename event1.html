BUND 1st Congress: Health Worker’s Day

The BUND 1st Congress: Health Worker’s Day was an inspiring and impactful gathering dedicated to honoring the dedication and resilience of health workers. This landmark event brought together healthcare professionals, policy-makers, and community leaders to celebrate the vital role health workers play in our society, especially in light of recent global challenges.
